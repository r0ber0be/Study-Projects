200 years of history

At a point that advances over the sea, later known as Ponta do Calabouço, between Piaçaba and Santa Luzia beaches, in the historic center of Rio de Janeiro, in 1603 the Portuguese built the Fortaleza de Santiago, the origin of the architectural complex that now houses the Museum National History.
